# Restaurant Owners / Managers — Questionnaire

This questionnaire is in two parts. **Part 1** asks the questions that are most
critical for our financial planning — just 5 quick ones. **Part 2** goes deeper
into your experience and would be incredibly valuable, but you've already helped
a lot if you stop after Part 1.

## Part 1: Essential (5 questions — ~3 minutes)

1. What's your average order value?

2. How many delivery orders do you get per day on average? What % of your total
   business is that?

3. What commission rate are you currently paying? What would you consider fair?

4. How long does it take to prepare a typical delivery order? Does it vary a lot
   by dish or during rush hours?

5. What would make you switch to a new platform? What's your single biggest
   frustration with current ones?

## Part 2: Helpful context (optional — ~10 minutes)

### Background (continued)

8. How long have you been using delivery platforms (Wolt, efood, etc.)?

9. Did you do delivery before the platforms existed (phone orders, own drivers)?

### Platform Economics (continued)

10. Are there any other fees (monthly subscription, listing fee, marketing
    fees)?

11. How and when does the platform pay you? Do you ever have disputes about the
    amount?

### Onboarding & Operations

12. How did you first hear about the platform? What convinced you to join?

13. What did the onboarding process look like? Was it easy?

14. Do you use the platform's tablet or your own device? How does managing
    incoming orders work for you?

15. Do you also use the platform's POS integration or do you punch orders in
    manually?

### Order Preparation & Efficiency (continued)

16. Do you find yourself prioritising certain menu items for delivery vs dine-in
    (e.g., simplifying the menu)?

17. Do you limit the number of concurrent delivery orders during peak times?
    How?

### Incidents & Logistics

18. What happens when a delivery is late, the food arrives cold, or an item is
    missing? Who bears the cost?

19. What happens when a customer complains? How does the platform handle it?

20. Do couriers ever cancel after arriving? How often does that happen?

21. How do you handle orders during peak hours when you're overwhelmed? Can you
    pause / turn off the platform?

### Marketing & Customer Acquisition

22. Do you feel the platform brings you new customers you wouldn't otherwise
    have? Roughly how many?

23. Do you run promotions or vouchers on the platform? Who pays for them?

### Support & Relationship

24. Do you have a dedicated account manager or contact at the platform? How
    often do you hear from them?

25. What happens when something goes wrong with the tablet or software? How
    quickly is it resolved?

26. If you have an issue with a specific courier or delivery, who do you
    contact? How responsive are they?

### Cooperative Model

27. How important is the commission rate vs other factors (reliability, customer
    base, courier quality)?

28. Would you be interested in a platform where you are a member-owner with a
    share of any surplus?

29. What would give you confidence to join a new, smaller platform at launch?

30. Is there anything else the platform could do (beyond commission) that would
    make your life easier?
